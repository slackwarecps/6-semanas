Claude uses the wrong skill or gets confused between similar skills. What is the cause and fix?
[Resposta correta: Confusão entre skills vem de descriptions parecidas demais, resolvida tornando-as mais distintas e específicas. Remover skills (B) elimina funcionalidade sem resolver a causa, o effort (C) não influencia isso e renomear com números (D) não distingue o propósito semântico.]

---
[ ] A - The descriptions are too similar; make them more distinct and specific to avoid conflicts.
[ ] B - There are too many skills installed; remove half of them.
[ ] C - The effort is too high; reduce it to low.
[ ] D - The skills need to be renamed with sequential numbers.
