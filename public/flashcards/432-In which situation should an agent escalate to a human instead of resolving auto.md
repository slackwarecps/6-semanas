In which situation should an agent escalate to a human instead of resolving autonomously?
[Resposta correta: A escalada correta ocorre diante de uma lacuna genuína de política que exige julgamento humano, com critérios explícitos definidos no prompt. Escalar por sentimento do cliente (A), por confiança autorreportada mal calibrada (B) ou por especulação sobre intenção futura (D) são exatamente as pegadinhas a evitar.]

---
[ ] A - When the customer seems angry or dissatisfied in the tone of the message.
[ ] B - When the model itself reports low confidence in the answer.
[ ] C - When there is a genuine policy gap (the policy is silent on the case) that requires human judgment.
[ ] D - When the agent speculates that the customer may have a problematic future intent.
