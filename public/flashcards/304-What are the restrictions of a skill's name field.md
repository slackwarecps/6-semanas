What are the restrictions of a skill's name field?
[Resposta correta: O name aceita só minúsculas, números e hifens, com no máximo 64 caracteres. A A confunde com o limite de description; C e D erram os caracteres e o limite.]

---
[ ] A - Any character, up to 1,024 characters
[ ] B - Only lowercase letters, numbers and hyphens; maximum of 64 characters
[ ] C - Uppercase letters and spaces allowed, no length limit
[ ] D - Only numbers, up to 32 characters
