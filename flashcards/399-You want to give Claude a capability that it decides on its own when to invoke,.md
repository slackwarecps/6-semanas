You want to give Claude a capability that it decides on its own when to invoke, such as executing JavaScript. Which primitive should you use?
[Resposta correta: Tools são controladas pelo modelo, que decide quando invocá-las. A e B têm outros controladores (aplicação e usuário). D não é um primitivo.]

---
[ ] A - Resources, controlled by the application.
[ ] B - Prompts, controlled by the user.
[ ] C - Tools, controlled by the model, since it is Claude that decides when to call them.
[ ] D - Client Session, which invokes the capability automatically.
