In a multiagent system, entire domains are missing from the final result. What is the most likely root cause?
[Resposta correta: A causa raiz costuma ser a decomposição estreita do coordenador. A pegadinha em A é culpar subagentes que executaram corretamente o escopo dado. C e D atribuem a fatores técnicos que não são o problema de escopo.]

---
[ ] A - The subagents executed the assigned tasks incorrectly.
[ ] B - The coordinator's task decomposition was too narrow and did not cover all domains.
[ ] C - The subagents' context window was small.
[ ] D - The model used had too few parameters.
