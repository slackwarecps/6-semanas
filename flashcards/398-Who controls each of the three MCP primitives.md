Who controls each of the three MCP primitives?
[Resposta correta: Tools são controladas pelo modelo, Resources pela aplicação e Prompts pelo usuário. A e D embaralham os controladores. C atribui tudo ao modelo, ignorando as camadas distintas.]

---
[ ] A - Tools by the user, Resources by the model, Prompts by the application.
[ ] B - Tools by the model/Claude, Resources by the application and Prompts by the user.
[ ] C - All three are controlled by the model.
[ ] D - Tools by the application, Resources by the user, Prompts by the model.
